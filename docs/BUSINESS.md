# Business & Growth Notes

> Early working notes on how Vibe Coding 24/7 sustains itself and grows.
> Nothing here is launched; this captures owner intent and open questions.
> Last updated: 2026-07-13.

## Revenue direction

### 1. Metropolis mall — storefront leasing (owner-stated)

The Neon Metropolis becomes a huge 3D shopping mall offering **real
products in real storefronts that people lease**.

- **The product**: a storefront unit inside the mall — 3D retail space a
  tenant configures with their branding and product displays.
- **v1 shape (proposed)**: tenants get a templated storefront (name,
  colors, logo-ish signage within our procedural constraints, product
  cards). Purchases link OUT to the tenant's own checkout (Shopify, Gumroad,
  etc.) — we don't process tenant payments at first.
- **Open questions**: lease pricing/terms, application vs. self-serve,
  content standards for kid-safe browsing, how foot traffic gets measured
  and reported to tenants (the stats engine can count zone visits).

### 2. Possible later lanes (not committed)

- Sponsored realm events or branded attractions (Resort rides, Terminal
  experiences).
- Benchmark-tier sponsorships in the Wonders (hardware brands love
  benchmarks).
- Featured kiosk placement for app makers.

## What stays free

- Visiting, exploring, playing community apps, accounts, chat, and voice.
  The community layer is the product's heart and stays free.

## Growth loops

1. **Builders bring audiences**: every submitted app is a creator with an
   audience who gets a shareable "my game lives HERE" 3D destination.
2. **The benchmark loop**: Wonders max-tier is inherently shareable
   ("can your PC run this in a browser?") — scores/FPS readouts make it
   competitive.
3. **Hangout gravity**: proximity voice + presence make the site a place
   to BE, not just view. 24/7 branding = someone is always online.
4. **Commerce foot traffic**: mall tenants promote their own storefronts,
   bringing shoppers who discover the rest of the universe.

## Constraints to respect

- **Kids visit.** Casino side of the Resort is entertainment-only (no real
  gambling/wagering); mall content must be kid-safe browsable; accounts
  stay minimal-PII (handle + password, no email).
- **No copyrighted game content** in the Console Realm — original
  reenactments/reimaginings only.
- Costs stay near-zero until revenue exists: one Railway service, no
  third-party realtime SaaS, no asset CDNs.
