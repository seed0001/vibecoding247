# Vision — Vibe Coding 24/7

> What this project is, why it exists, and where it's going.
> Last updated: 2026-07-13.

## The one-liner

**vibecoding247.net is a first-person 3D universe where the vibe-coding
community lives, plays, and ships.** Not a link directory with a 3D skin —
a place. You drop in, walk around, meet people, and discover games and apps
built by other vibe coders.

## Why it exists

Vibe coders are shipping thousands of small apps and games across Vercel,
Railway, HF Spaces, and everywhere else — and almost all of them vanish into
the feed within a day. There's no *place* where that creative output lives,
gets discovered, and gets celebrated. Vibe Coding 24/7 is that place: every
approved app gets a physical home inside a navigable 3D world.

## The core loop

1. **Visit** — land on the site, get dropped first-person into the Nexus.
2. **Explore** — five pedestals warp to five realms, each an infinite
   themed world holding community apps at 3D kiosks.
3. **Play** — launch any app right from its kiosk.
4. **Meet** — see other explorers as presence orbs (avatars later), chat by
   text, talk by proximity voice. The site is a hangout, not a catalog.
5. **Submit** — ship your own app, get a spot in a realm, come watch people
   find it.

## What makes it different

- **First-person everything.** No menus-first design. The world IS the UI.
- **The Nexus joke**: a deliberately small, richly detailed room where five
  tiny objects open into infinite worlds.
- **Real multiplayer** on a hobby-site budget: presence, chat, and
  spatialized WebRTC voice, all self-hosted on one Railway service.
- **Every realm has its own identity and its own ambition** (see
  [REALMS.md](REALMS.md)) — a console realm, a resort/theme park, a real
  shopping mall, a graphics-benchmark nature preserve, and a procedural
  galaxy.
- **No gatekeeping aesthetic**: built BY vibe coding, FOR vibe coders. The
  whole site is itself the proof that vibe coding ships real things.

## Long-term direction

- The realms grow infinitely — capacity is never the constraint.
- Social layer deepens: custom avatars (mirror customization), rewards,
  collectibles, hidden areas, rabbit holes.
- Real commerce enters through the Metropolis mall (leased storefronts).
- The site becomes a destination people return to daily — 24/7 is the name
  and the goal: someone is always in the world.

## Owner's north star

"Very realistic realism type stuff" — every space should keep climbing
toward the detail bar the Nexus set, one area at a time. Ship fast, test
live, keep moving.
